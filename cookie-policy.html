Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how abdurrahmannawar.me uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website. It explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control their use.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, improve user experience, and provide reporting information.

How We Use Cookies

We only use Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics cookies collect information such as:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • Device and browser type
  • General geographic location

This data is anonymous and used solely to improve the performance and content of our website.

Your Choices

You can choose to disable or delete cookies in your browser settings at any time. Most browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies you have
  • Delete them
  • Block them entirely or from specific sites

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking specifically, you can use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
